RESOURCES 4 VRCHAT

what's this?

this is a list of various resources for VRChat that you may find useful or interesting!

world building resources

[VRC Prefabs]
Community curated list of free drag-and-drop prefabs for your VRChat worlds.

[LTCGI]
Fast Realtime-GI shader (requires specific shaders to be used for your world's textures).

[Kanikama]
Realtime-GI shader (does not require specific shaders to be used for your world's textures).

avatar creation resources

[404DOTEXE]
Free source for VRoid fashion.

[Avatar Network (JP)]
Aggregates clothing for avatars from booth.pm, and groups them per avatar. List is sorted by amount of clothing available for each given avatar.

community resources

[VRChat Party Hub]
Curated daily roundup of most VRChat music events.

[VR Event Calendar (JP)]
Curated calendar of Japanese VRChat events.
